Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my Apple Music queue?

Open QueueMaster and tap the "Sync Queue" button on the main screen. Make sure you have granted the app permission to access your Apple Music library.

Why can't I see my queue?

Ensure that Apple Music is playing and that you have an active Apple Music subscription. The app requires an active subscription to access your queue data.

How does Queue Protection work?

Queue Protection automatically saves your queue at regular intervals. If the app crashes or your device restarts, you can restore your queue from the History tab.

Can I edit my queue directly in the app?

Yes! You can reorder songs by dragging them, remove songs by swiping left, and add songs from your library using the search feature.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. QueueMaster only accesses your music queue data and does not collect or store any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.

Troubleshooting

App not syncing? Try force-closing and reopening the app, or restart your device.

Music not playing? Check that Apple Music is installed and you have an active subscription.

Performance issues? Ensure your device is running the latest iOS version and has sufficient storage space.
