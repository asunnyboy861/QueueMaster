Privacy Policy

QueueMaster - Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: March 15, 2024

Introduction

QueueMaster ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application QueueMaster (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the App. The only information you may voluntarily provide is when you contact us for support via email.

Automatically Collected Information

The App accesses the following information from your device solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Apple Music Library Access: The App accesses your Apple Music queue to display and manage your current playlist. This access is read-only and is used solely for the purpose of queue management.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as device model and iOS version may be collected anonymously for crash reporting and app improvement purposes.

Important: QueueMaster does not collect, store, or transmit your personal music preferences, listening history, or any other personal data to our servers. All queue data remains on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • To respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • To improve the App's performance and user experience
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or crashes

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All queue data, settings, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and local files). We do not store your data on external servers.

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple MusicKit: Used to access your Apple Music queue. Apple's privacy practices apply to this integration. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases (if applicable). Apple's privacy practices apply.

We do not integrate with any analytics services, advertising networks, or social media platforms that would collect your personal data.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can access all your data stored within the App at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the App or using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings.
  • Permissions: You can revoke the App's access to Apple Music at any time through your device Settings > Privacy & Security > Media & Apple Music.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
